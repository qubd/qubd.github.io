Who Am I?

I'm Brendan. I teach mathematics in Montreal. I enjoy racing bicycles up hills and around muddy parks. You can contact me at brendan.cordy@gmail.com, and you can find a link to my github page over there on the right.

Mathematics

As a graduate student, I spent a lot of time thinking about mathematical logic and category theory. I've also studied formal languages, computability & complexity, and cryptography.

Nowadays I'm often thinking about statistics, especially the nonparametric kind, as well as high school and undergrad mathematics contest problems. If you're interested in mathematics contests at the CEGEP level, you might find some nice resources in the links section.

Papers, programs, interactive demonstrations, and various other mathematical things that I've created for my own benefit, but you might find interesting as well, are available in the projects & papers section.
