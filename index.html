Who Am I?

I'm Brendan. I teach mathematics in Montreal. I devote some of my energy to homebrewing and cycling. You can contact me at myfirstname.mylastname@gmail.com, and you can find my github page here.

Mathematics

I've spent a lot of time thinking about mathematical logic and category theory. I've also studied formal languages, computability & complexity, and cryptography.

Nowadays, I like to pick a good text in an area I don't know much about and spend a while learning something new. Elliptic curves and Bayesian statistics are things I'm currently interested in.
